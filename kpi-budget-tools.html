In this budget plan, the focus is on the practical allocation of $965 across critical IT support areas to enhance performance and professional development. The ITIL Foundation exam prepares Tier 2 technicians for structured service delivery. Ergonomic accessories like headsets and mice contribute to clearer communication and better support quality.
